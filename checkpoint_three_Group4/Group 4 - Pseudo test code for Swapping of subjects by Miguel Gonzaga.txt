# Test Script for Swapping a Subject in the Enrollment System

– Test 1: Verify Subject Availability

1. Log in to the Enrollment system
2. Navigate to the list of enrolled subjects
3. Verify that the subject (ISDEVOPS - G306A - 1240H to 1415H) is currently enrolled

– Test 2: Swap Subject

4. Click on the "swap" button on the list of options
5. Select the subject (ISDEVOPS - G306A - 1240H to 1415H) to be swapped
6. Select the new subject (ISSECUR - G306B - 1430H to 1600H) to be swapped
7. Click confirm
8. Verify that a confirmation message is displayed
9. Confirm the swap
10. Navigate to the list of enrolled subjects
11. Verify that the item (ISDEVOPS - G306A - 1240H to 1415H) is no longer enrolled
12. Verify that the item (ISSECUR - G306B - 1430H to 1600H) is now enrolled

– Test 3: Verify Updated Availability

13. Log in to the Enrollment system
14. Navigate to the list of available subjects
15. Verify that the subject (ISDEVOPS) has a room (G306B) available at the schedule (1430H to 1600H)
